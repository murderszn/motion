# Composition Guide

## Aspect Ratio Selection

| Ratio | Best Presets | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **1:1** | All presets | Instagram feed, profile |
| **16:9** | Flow, Waves, Aurora | YouTube, desktop wallpaper |
| **9:16** | Aurora, Electric, Reeded | TikTok, Reels, Stories |
| **4:5** | Halftone, Grain | Instagram portrait |

## Loop Duration Guide

| Duration | Best Use |
|----------|----------|
| 2–3s | Punchy social clips, GIFs. Needs high speed. |
| 4–5s | Most natural loop (default). Good for all presets. |
| 6–8s | Slow, meditative. Low speed recommended. |
| 9–10s | Very slow evolves. Wallpaper material. |

## Mood Mapping

| Mood | Presets | Palette | Speed |
|------|---------|---------|-------|
| Calm | Flow, Waves, Orbs | Ocean, Stone | 0.20–0.40 |
| Dramatic | Electric, Aurora, Glass | Crimson, Violet | 0.40–0.60 |

| Elegant | Reeded, Grain, Glass | Noir, Rose | 0.20–0.40 |
| Psychedelic | Flow, Kaleidoscope, Plasma | Acid, Gold | 0.60–1.00 |
