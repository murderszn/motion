# Kaleidoscope

**Index:** 9 | **Icon:** KA

Mirrored radial symmetry slices creating a kaleidoscopic effect.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Still | Slow rotation | Moderate spin | Fast spinning | Rapid whirling |
| scale | ~2 wide slices | ~4 slices | ~6 slices | ~8 fine slices | ~12 very tight |
| density | Faint pattern | Soft blend | Moderate intensity | Strong contrast | Very vivid |
| distortion | Perfect symmetry | Slight warp | Noticeable bending | Strong deformation | Extreme distortion |
| detail | Smooth surfaces | Subtle texture | Moderate detail | Rich texture | Maximum complexity |

## Sweet Spot

`scale 0.40–0.70` for readable symmetry. `distortion 0.20–0.50` keeps kaleidoscope structure recognizable.

## Best Palettes

acid (6), gold (8), violet (3)
