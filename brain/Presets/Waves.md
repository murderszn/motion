# Waves

**Index:** 3 | **Icon:** WV

Flowing contour bands — topographic map lines in motion.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Frozen | Slow drift | Moderate flow | Fast rippling | Rapid motion |
| scale | ~1.5 broad bands | 2.5 bands | ~3.5 bands | ~4.5 fine bands | ~5.0 very tight |
| density | ~3 crests | ~6 crests | ~9 crests | ~12 crests | ~14+ dense |
| distortion | Clean sine waves | Slight bending | Noticeable warp | Strong distortion | Very broken organic |
| detail | Smooth bands | Subtle texture | Moderate detail | Sharp edges | Very sharp contrast |

## Sweet Spot

`scale 0.40–0.70` + `density 0.30–0.60` for cleanest topo-band look. High distortion makes it organic/fluid.

## Best Palettes

crimson (0), gold (8), ocean (4)
