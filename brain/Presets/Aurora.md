# Aurora

**Index:** 7 | **Icon:** AU

Wavy glowing curtains of light undulating across a dark night sky.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Curtains frozen | Slow undulation | Moderate wave | Active waving | Rapid flickering |
| scale | Broad tall curtains | Moderate height | Balanced | Shorter tight | Very short compact |
| density | Wide diffuse bands | Moderate width | Balanced | Tight bands | Very narrow sharp |
| distortion | Clean curtains | Subtle warp | Moderate | Strong warp broken | Very fragmented |
| detail | Smooth curtains | Mild striations | Noticeable streaks | Strong striations | Intricate veining |

## Sweet Spot

`speed 0.20–0.50` for graceful movement. `density 0.40–0.70` + `detail 0.50–0.80` for realistic curtain texture. Dark palettes work best.

## Best Palettes

aurora (1), violet (3), cobalt (9)
