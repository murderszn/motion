# Grain

**Index:** 5 | **Icon:** GR

Soft grainy gradient washes.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Lights frozen | Slow orbit | Moderate drift | Fast orbiting | Rapid sweeping |
| scale | Very broad gradient | Wide soft blends | Moderate | Tighter patterns | Fine detail structure |
| density | Very faint wash | Gentle light | Moderate intensity | Strong light | Very bright saturated |
| distortion | No grain | Subtle grain | Moderate noise | Heavy grain | Extreme grit |
| detail | Very smooth | Subtle texture | Moderate | Pronounced features | Very rich texture |

## Sweet Spot

`speed 0.30–0.50` + `density 0.40–0.60` for smoothest washes. Keep `distortion ≤0.40` and `grain ≤0.30` for clean looks.

## Best Palettes

noir (2), rose (7), stone (10)
