# Halftone

**Index:** 4 | **Icon:** HT

Animated dot-grid over a flow field.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Dots static | Slow drift | Moderate flow | Fast dot animation | Rapid motion |
| scale | Very zoomed out | Broad patterns | Moderate | Fine flow detail | Very intricate |
| density | ~18 large dots | ~34 dots | ~50 dots | ~65 small dots | ~80 tiny dots |
| distortion | Minimal warp | Gentle bending | Noticeable | Strong flow warp | Extreme deformation |
| detail | Very large dots | Moderate size | Balanced | Small dots | Tiny dots sparse |

## Sweet Spot

`density 0.50–0.80` + `detail 0.30–0.60` for readable halftone. Low detail = large connected dots, high detail = tiny isolated dots.

## Best Palettes

noir (2), acid (6), stone (10)
