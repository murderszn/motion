# Glass

**Index:** 6 | **Icon:** GL

Glassmorphism — drifting frosted panes refracting a coloured backdrop.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Panes frozen | Slow drift | Moderate orbit | Active drifting | Fast panes |
| scale | Very large panes | Broad panes | Moderate | Smaller panes | Many small panes |
| density | Small separated | Moderate size | Balanced | Larger panes | Very large overlapping |
| distortion | No refraction | Subtle lens | Noticeable bending | Strong refraction | Extreme distortion |
| detail | No rim light | Subtle rim glow | Moderate rim | Bright highlights | Very bright prominent |

## Sweet Spot

`distortion 0.30–0.60` for best glass illusion. `detail 0.40–0.70` makes pane edges pop with rim highlights.

## Best Palettes

crimson (0), violet (3), ember (5)
