# Plasma

**Index:** 11 | **Icon:** PL

Classic multi-frequency sine wave plasma pattern.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Frozen pattern | Slow morph | Moderate flow | Fast wave | Rapid motion |
| scale | ~2 broad waves | ~4 wave bands | ~6 bands | ~8 fine bands | ~10+ dense |
| density | Very faint | Soft plasma | Moderate | Strong color | Very saturated |
| distortion | Clean sine | Gentle warp | Noticeable mix | Strong interference | Extreme complexity |
| detail | 2 frequencies | 3 frequencies | Balanced | 5+ frequencies | Maximum overlap |

## Sweet Spot

`scale 0.40–0.70` + `detail 0.40–0.70` for recognizable plasma with good colour range.
