# Reeded Glass

**Index:** 0 | **Icon:** RD

Vertical reeded-glass rods refracting drifting color blobs. The original inspiration preset.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Almost still | Gentle drift | Moderate orbit | Fast swirling | Rapid, chaotic |
| scale | Large, few features | Broad undulations | Balanced | Finer texture | Very fine detail |
| density | ~24 wide rods | ~45 rods | ~65 rods | ~85 thin rods | ~110 hairline rods |
| distortion | Flat rods | Gentle refraction | Noticeable bending | Strong lens | Extreme refraction |
| detail | Smooth surfaces | Subtle variation | Moderate texture | Pronounced texture | Very rough, noisy |

## Sweet Spot

`density 0.40–0.70` + `distortion 0.40–0.80` for classic reeded glass. Low speed `0.20–0.40` makes refraction feel elegant.
