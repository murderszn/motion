# Melt

**Index:** 13 | **Icon:** ME

Vertical dripping liquid distortion effect.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Still | Slow drip | Moderate flow | Fast dripping | Rapid melting |
| scale | Very large drips | Broad distortion | Moderate | Fine streaks | Very fine detail |
| density | Faint effect | Soft drips | Noticeable | Strong drip pattern | Dense dripping |
| distortion | Minimal warp | Gentle stretch | Clear drip | Strong vertical stretch | Extreme melt |
| detail | Smooth flow | Subtle variation | Moderate texture | Pronounced streaks | Very complex |

## Sweet Spot

`distortion 0.40–0.70` + `speed 0.30–0.60` for natural-looking melt effect.

## Best Palettes

ember (5), rose (7), violet (3)
