# Electric

**Index:** 8 | **Icon:** EL

Branching, flickering lightning filaments.

## Slider Outcomes

| Slider | 0.0–0.2 | 0.2–0.4 | 0.4–0.6 | 0.6–0.8 | 0.8–1.0 |
|--------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| speed | Still bolts | Slow flicker | Moderate | Fast flickering | Rapid chaotic flash |
| scale | Very large broad | Large branches | Moderate | Fine intricate | Very fine dense |
| density | Dim ambient haze | Subtle glow | Moderate | Bright glow | Very bright saturated |
| distortion | Straight filaments | Gentle bend | Moderate warp | Strong curves | Extreme twisting |
| detail | ~6 power thick | ~10 power | ~15 power | ~20 power fine | ~24 power very thin |

## Sweet Spot

`detail 0.50–0.80` for visible branching. `distortion 0.40–0.60` keeps bolts readable. Low speed `0.20–0.40` for drama.

## Best Palettes

ember (5), acid (6), cobalt (9)
