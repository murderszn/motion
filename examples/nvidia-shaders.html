NVIDIA GPU Gems — Shader Examples

Interactive WebGL implementations of techniques from the GPU Gems series: Perlin noise, depth of field, motion blur, and procedural textures.

WebGL 1.0 · GLSL ES 1.0 · No dependencies

01 Improved Perlin Noise

Ken Perlin's quintic interpolation with Stefan Gustavson's textureless implementation. Quintic curve eliminates banding artifacts found in cubic hermite.

GPU Gems 2 & 3 · Chapter 26 · Textureless Procedural Noise

02 Turbulence

fBM with absolute value of noise at each octave. Creates sharp valleys and flame-like ridges — the "turbulence" variant of fractal noise.

GPU Gems 2 · Chapter 26 · fBM Turbulence Variant

03 Ridged Multi-Fractal

Sharp mountain ridges and veins. Inverts and squares noise at each octave to create crisp ridgelines.

GPU Gems 2 · Chapter 26 · Ridged Multi-Fractal Noise

04 Marble Texture

Domain-warped sine wave modulated by fBM noise. The noise displaces the phase of the sine, creating natural veining patterns.

GPU Gems · Procedural Texturing · Marble Recipe

05 Domain Warping

Feed fBM output back into its own input: f(p + fBM(p + fBM(p))). Creates organic, fluid distortion — clouds, marble, swirling turbulence.

GPU Gems · Inigo Quilez · Domain Warping Technique

06 Circle of Confusion (DoF)

Depth of field simulation using Circle of Confusion. Pixels far from the focus plane get variable-width blur. Simulates real camera lens optics.

GPU Gems 3 · Chapter 28 · Practical Depth of Field

07 Separable Gaussian Blur

NVIDIA's bilinear optimization: sample between texel centers to halve texture fetches. Uses GPU's hardware bilinear unit for free blending.

GPU Gems 2 & 3 · Separable Bilinear Sampling Optimization

08 Volumetric Light Scattering

Screen-space radial blur simulating light shafts (god rays). Samples along a ray from the pixel towards the light source, accumulating occlusion and intensity.

GPU Gems 3 · Chapter 13 · Volumetric Light Scattering

09 Fresnel Reflection & Refraction

Ray-traced procedural sphere calculating specular reflection and index-based refraction. Blends refraction and reflection using Schlick's approximation.

GPU Gems · Chapter 16 · Real-Time Refraction

10 Gerstner Wave Synthesis

Water wave heights synthesized by summing directional sinusoids. Computes normals analytically to render a reflective ocean surface with specular highlights.

GPU Gems · Chapter 1 · Effective Water Simulation

11 Flow Map & Fluid Advection

Fluid flow advection using a dynamic vector field. Combines dual out-of-phase texture coordinates to loop flow indefinitely without infinite stretching.

GPU Gems 2 · Chapter 18 · Flow Map Rendering

12 Chromatic Dispersion

Spectral split of refracted light inside a faceted glass gem. Evaluates separate refractive indices (eta) for Red, Green, and Blue light rays.

GPU Gems · Chapter 16 & 19 · Refractive Dispersion

13 Subsurface Scattering

Backlit volumetric light scattering approximation. Simulates translucency and internal light transport inside materials like skin, wax, or jade.

GPU Gems 3 · Chapter 14 · Subsurface Light Transport

14 Parallax Occlusion Mapping

Procedural height-raymarching inside a fragment shader. Evaluates height offsets and calculates self-shadowing to render realistic 3D crevices on a flat plane.

GPU Gems 3 · Chapter 18 · Parallax Displacement

15 Lens Flare & Starbursts

Dynamic camera lens artifacts including chromatic halos, light ray diffraction, and camera ghosts aligned symmetrically along the light vector.

GPU Gems 2 · Chapter 27 · Advanced Image Processing

16 Volumetric Fog & Clouds

Raymarched 3D-like volume simulating light absorption and scattering through multiple layers of noise. Accumulates optical density based on Beer's Law.

GPU Gems 3 · Chapter 30 · Real-Time Volumetric Clouds
