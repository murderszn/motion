Usage Warnings & Care Guidelines

01 / Washing Protocol

Wash inside out in cold water with similar colors. Do not iron directly on the inkblot graphic. Hang dry to maintain the structural integrity of the high-density screen print.

02 / Symmetrical Resonance

Due to the generative nature of the print, observers may report varying visual forms (moths, wolves, dancing figures, or abstract landscapes). Symmetrical patterns are psychologically safe for standard optical exposure.

03 / Sustainable Logistics

Our packaging is 100% biodegradable cornstarch compostable mailers. All shipments are carbon neutral. Formulated in Portugal. Designed in the Void.
